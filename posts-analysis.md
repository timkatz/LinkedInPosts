# LinkedIn Posts Performance Analysis

**Analysis Date:** December 27, 2025
**Data Period:** August 17 - December 21, 2025
**Total Posts Analyzed:** 34

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis examines 34 LinkedIn posts over a 4+ month period to identify patterns in engagement, optimal posting times, and content themes that resonate with the target audience of eCommerce brands, fashion brands, and CEOs/founders.

**Important Context:** On October 30, 2025, a strategy shift occurred from ghostwritten content to Tim's authentic voice. This analysis covers both periods:
- **Ghostwriter period (Aug 17 - Oct 26):** 14 posts, 3,563 avg impressions
- **Authentic voice period (Nov 2 - Dec 21):** 20 posts, 1,131 avg impressions

**Key Findings:**
- Best performing day: Tuesday (1,471 avg impressions in authentic voice period)
- Best posting time: 9-10 AM PST (most consistent engagement)
- Top post: Shopify migration timelines (Sep 25) - 185 reactions, 31,477 impressions, 31 comments
- Second best: Migration phases (Dec 14) - 13 reactions, 4,429 impressions
- Third best: Shopify app audit (Dec 9) - 39 reactions, 4,254 impressions
- Viral threshold: 10,000+ impressions (1 post achieved this)
- Strong threshold: 4,000+ impressions (3 posts achieved this)
- Engagement rate range: 0-4% (average 1.8% across all posts)
- **Migration content consistently outperforms** all other themes

## Content Theme Performance Patterns

### What Correlates with High Performance

**Migration Content (STRONGEST THEME):**
- Shopify migration timelines (31,477 impressions) - 3-6 month roadmap
- Migration phases explained (4,429 impressions) - Phase breakdown
- Shopify app audit (4,254 impressions) - Migration-adjacent optimization
- **Pattern:** 3 of top 4 posts are migration-related. This is the proven winning theme.

**Tactical + Timeline:**
- Shopify migration (31,477 impressions) - 3-6 month roadmap
- Migration phases (4,429 impressions) - Phase 1/2/3 breakdown
- Agency 30-day plan (1,420 impressions) - weekly breakdown
- **Pattern:** Specific timeframes with step-by-step breakdown

**Specific Numbers in Hook:**
- "18-22 apps installed" (4,254 impressions)
- "3-6 months" (31,477 impressions)
- "82% email revenue increase" (3,371 impressions)
- **Pattern:** Posts with concrete numbers in the first line outperform by 3-5x

**Credibility Markers:**
- PacSun $100M+ (3,741 impressions)
- "After reviewing thousands..." (1,370 impressions)
- "50+ Shopify clients" (4,254 impressions)
- **Pattern:** Quantified experience beats generic claims

**Before/After Results:**
- 0.8% ‚Üí 3.1% conversion (1,030 impressions)
- 18 apps ‚Üí 5 apps (4,254 impressions)

- **Pattern:** Clear transformation with mechanism explained

**Agency Accountability:**
- Agency excuses (1,420 impressions)

## Audience Behavior Insights

### What the Data Reveals

**1. Migration Content is the Winning Formula**
- Shopify migration (Sep 25): 31,477 impressions (viral)
- Migration phases (Dec 14): 4,429 impressions (strong)
- Shopify app audit (Dec 9): 4,254 impressions (strong)
- **Takeaway:** 3 of top 4 posts are migration-related. Double down on this theme.

**2. Urgency Drives Virality**
- Shopify migration (pre-holiday urgency): 31,477 impressions
- General retention advice: 690 impressions
- **Takeaway:** Timely > Timeless for reach

**3. Specificity Builds Trust**
- Posts with specific numbers in hook average 2,500+ impressions
- Posts without numbers average 700 impressions
- **Takeaway:** Lead with concrete metrics. "18-22 apps" beats "too many apps"

**4. Morning is for Business**
- 9-11 AM posts: 1,985 avg impressions
- 5-8 PM posts: 1,331 avg impressions
- **Takeaway:** Professional audience prioritizes LinkedIn in morning

**5. Tuesday Has Replaced Thursday**
- Ghostwriter period: Thursday was strongest (7,407 avg, skewed by viral post)
- Authentic voice period: Tuesday leads (1,471 avg vs Thursday 931)
- **Takeaway:** Test Tuesday as primary posting day in Q1 2026

**6. Controversy Needs Solution**
- CEO advice (with solution): 2,219 impressions
- Anti-guru (mostly critique): 1,325 impressions
- **Takeaway:** Criticism + alternative outperforms pure critique

**7. Short Posts Underperform**
- Posts under 150 words: 286 avg impressions
- Posts over 300 words: 1,847 avg impressions
- **Takeaway:** Longer tactical content works. Don't sacrifice depth for brevity.
